    About Nebula Coders

    Nebula Coders are the group of 7 Students from the B. P. Mandal College Of Engineering, Madhepura. Our College is recognized under AICTE and affiliated to Bihar Engineering University, Patna. We are updating ourself daily in this progressing World. We have students from Computer Science Department and Electronics Departments. Our skills are C,OOPS in Python and C++, Arduino Progarmming, Embedded Systems, database in MySQL and SQLite, PHP, WebDevelopement and its updating each day. Our main vision is to get ourselves skilled so that in near future our members achieve higher positions in different MNCs and Indian Companies.

    Founders' Desk

    We at Nebula Coders believe in practical knowledge, in-depth or hands-on experiences rather than the bookish theortical knowledge. Idea of Nebula Coders Pop-up in my mind from NEBULA present in the universe. In this generation of Artificial Intelligences like Gemini,Bing,GPT4, we believe in having the real world knowledge in the field of embedded Systems. I believe that embedded systems is the one who is controlling the maximum of world. We see embedded programmed gadgets from smartwatches to boilers and from cars to railways; Embedded systems is everwhere. We should look about embedded systems and its real world applications and try to find the scope in the field of embedded systems.
    -Nishant Raj
